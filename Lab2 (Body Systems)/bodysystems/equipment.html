Six key reasons why Body Systems equipment is built to last:

  • Body Systems uses only the highest quality materials and components available.
  • All equipment is built with durable, electrostatically applied epoxy powder coat finish to resist scratching, chipping and peeling.
  • Our pulley machines are built with impact resistant fiberglass reinforced nylon pulleys with sealed bearings.
  • Weight machines support a heavy-duty high tensile strength steel mainframe construction.
  • Support beams use four-side welded construction to keep torsional flex to a minimum for solid and stable workouts.
  • All weight macines use tear resistant pads and rollers feature high-density, top grade foam for safety, comfort and stability.
